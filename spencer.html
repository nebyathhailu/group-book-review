Who Moved My Cheese

Book Review

“Who Moved my Cheese?” is a simple story that reveals a simple profound truth about change.It’s a funny story of four characters who live in maze which can be related to the (world) we live in and look for cheese where cheese is a metaphor of the things one desires for and is searching for it can be (money, love, education,job) to keep themselves happy.


The story revolves around how each character reacts when an “unexpected change” in circumstances deprives them of the cheese that they love so dearly.Their reaction to the situation gives valuable lessons that can help you discover how to deal with change so as to enjoy less stress and more success in life.


The four characters( Sniff, Scurry, Hem and Haw) represent the simple and complex part of ourselves.

Sniff represents someone who sniffs out change early.This character embodies the ability to quickly perceive change and recognize the need for adoption.Sniff’s quick awareness of shifting allows a reaction to change and explore new possibilities.


Scurry represents someone who when they see change they quickly get into action and adapt to the change.It also represents a proactive approach to change, immediately taking action to adapt to the new circumstances.His eagerness to move forward and explore the maze reflect a willingness to embrace change.


Hem represents someone who fears and denies change.Hem’s character embodies resistance to change and fear of the unknown.Hem’s reluctance to leave the familiar and embrace the uncertainty of the maze highlights the challenge of adapting to change.


Haw represents someone who learns to adapt in time when he or she sees change.Initially hesitant like Hem, Haw represents the journey of overcoming resistance to change and embracing new possibilities.Haw’s eventual willingness to explore the maze demonstrates the potential for growth and adaptation when individuals overcome their fears.


The conversations between the characters in “Who Moved My Cheese?” serves as an example of how individuals navigate change in their own lives.The book emphasizes on the importance of being adaptable, proactive, and willing to let go of the past to embrace new opportunities.The character’s dialogue highlight the different strategies people employ when faced with unexpected shifts,offering valuable insights on how to approach change in a positive and productive way.

Embracing Change

The book emphasizes that change is inevitable and constant in life.In today’s world , businesses,individuals,learning institutions must adapt to new technologies , for learning institutions they should embrace the e-learning by using e-learning platforms and teaching via the e-learning platforms,shifting the market trends, and evolving the customer expectations.Embracing change allows for growth,innovation. And ability to get new opportunities.

Anticipate Change

Staying informed about potential shifts and change in an environment is crucial.This involves monitoring the trends in certain ways people communicate, market research and being aware of the emerging technologies.By anticipating change individuals and organizations can prepare for it , minimizing disruption and maximizing their ability to change.

Adapting Quickly to Change

The ability to adjust to changing circumstances is necessary.It means being flexible, willing to let go of old ways or behavior and being open to new approaches.In a competitive market the faster you adapt the better your chances of success.

Overcoming Fear

Fear of the unknown can hinder progress and prevent people from embracing new opportunities.The book highlights the importance of facing fears and stepping into the unknown.This involves taking calculated risks, seeking new skills and pushing beyond comfort zones.

Enjoying the Journey of Change

The book encourages a positive attitude towards change ,rather than being afraid of change. Learning to positively take the process of adaptation, celebrating small victories and finding joy in the journey can make change less discouraging and more rewarding.

The quicker you let go of old cheese, the sooner you find the new cheese.
